# Privacy Policy

  **Last Updated: November 2, 2025**

  ## 1. Information We Collect

  This TikTok Uploader application ("the App") operates locally on your
  device.

  ### Information Stored Locally:
  - TikTok account authentication tokens
  - Profile settings and preferences
  - Application configuration

  ### Information NOT Collected:
  - We do not collect or store your personal information on external servers
  - We do not track your usage
  - We do not sell your data to third parties

  ## 2. How We Use Your Information

  The authentication tokens are used solely to:
  - Authenticate with TikTok's official API
  - Upload videos to your TikTok account on your behalf

  ## 3. Data Storage

  All data is stored locally on your device. Authentication tokens are
  stored in:
  - `tiktok_profiles.json` (encrypted)

  ## 4. Third-Party Services

  This App uses:
  - **TikTok API**: Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy
  (https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy)

  ## 5. Data Security

  We implement appropriate security measures to protect your authentication
  tokens.

  ## 6. Your Rights

  You can:
  - Delete your stored profiles at any time through the App
  - Revoke access through TikTok's settings

  ## 7. Changes to Privacy Policy

  We may update this policy. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance
   of changes.
